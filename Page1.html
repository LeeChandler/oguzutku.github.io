Biography Of Bill Gates

William Henry Gates III, or his most known name Bill Gates was born in 1955.He is a technologist,philantropist and business leader.He first met a computer in 1967 and that changed his life.Bill and his chilhood friend Paul Allen spent their spare time with working on computer programming.After a while, Bill entered Harvard University through his father’s advice.Bill had a marriage with Melinda French in 1994.They have three children.After 6 years of this marriage, Bill and Melinda founded a charitable foundation together: Bill And Melinda Gates Foundation.That is one of the biggest charity foundation ever.

Bill and his two sisters grew up in Seattle.His father, William H.Gates II, is an attorney and his late mother, Mary Gates was a schoolteacher.When he was 12, he went to his first information course.In that days, Bill started writing own programs.After a while, Bill and Paul started to finding software mistakes for a company to use its computer for free.At 1973, Bill enrolled in the Harvard as a pre-law major.But his focus quickly ran towards the computer science.He dropped out of college to start Microsoft with Paul Allen at 1975.In fact, Bill and Paul took leave of absence from Harvard because they got an offer for developing software from MITS Altair.Bill and Paul registered the trademark ’’Microsoft’’ in 1976.After a year, Bill took his second leave of absence from Harvard, and sets up Microsoft in Albuquerque.After that, Microsoft’s profits was getting bigger day by day.He had a strict performance principle.That situation gave him a tough guy reputation.In 1981, Microsoft bought the rights to operating system so called “DOS” from Seattle Computer Products.The system was modified and renamed as “MS-DOS”. This system broke sales records in the 80’s and had international reputation.In parallel to its achievement in operating systems,Bill has also worked extremely well in application programs.In 1983, he launched an word processing system so called “WORD”.It soon reached success especially Europe.Microsoft’s definitive success in the software was made by the order that the Apple company gave them.They developed some aplication systems for Macintosh.At age of 31, Bill Gates became the youngest billionaire ever.
